Resources

Art of Problem Solving (AoPS)

An online math education site containing many helpful resources.

  • Contest collections contain a large amount of past competition problems.
  • AMC past tests also include multiple video and written solutions per probelm.
  • The AoPS problem generator provides random practice problems using the AoPS wiki.

Omega Learn

A site dedicated to collecting useful math resources.

  • A course designed to prepare for the AMC 10 and 12 competitions.

California Mathematics League (CAML)

A competition website which also hosts test samples.

  • Sample tests include only 1 high school sample, which can still be helpful to prepare.

CAMS Math Club

Serving all members by giving them opportunities to learn and explore mathematical topics.
