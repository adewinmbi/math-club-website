About Math Club

The Purpose of Math Club

Math club is committed to supporting every member's experience with math. Through competitions, teaching, and practicing in a team environment, we hope to inspire an interest for math in every student. Meetings may include:

  • Preparing for competitive math by taking practice tests.
  • A test-taking environment to participate in various competitions.
  • Explanations to difficult math problems provided by board members and others.

We hope math club will serve all members by giving them opportunities to learn and explore mathematical topics.

Competitions

Members of math club will have the opportunity to compete in various competitions to utilize and strengthen their skills.

  • 01 American Mathematics Competition (AMC 10/12)

    The AMC is a multiple choice exam designed to enhance problem solving skills. Members in grades 9-10 may participate in the AMC 10 or 12, while members in grades 1-12 may participate in the AMC 12. More information can be found here

  • 02 California Mathematics League (CAML)

    The CAML consists of multiple tests, each with six questions on topics ranging from Algebra to Analysis. Club members of all grade levels may participate in this competition. More information can be found here
